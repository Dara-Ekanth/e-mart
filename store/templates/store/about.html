We are 4th year young Computer Science and Engineering Under Graduate students. This project is developed for Net Centric Program an elective based subject in Amrita School of Engineering, Bengaluru, having Ms. Nalini Sampath as our faculty. We developed a web-based application in which a user can able to purchase the products from the store. And there are different categories of products that will be available, in which the user can able to choose a category and have to add the products to the cart. And after adding all the required products to the cart the user can able to pay the billed amount from the integrated payment gateway and an order ID will be created so that the user has to show the order ID at the store to pick his order. Users can able to shop from the comfort of their home. And we have created our custom wallet so that users can Top Up their wallet and can pay bill directly from the wallet. Users will get to know about the quality of the products by the reviews of the previous buyers. We have a statistics report showing for each of the product that guides the users to take a decision to buy the product or not.
